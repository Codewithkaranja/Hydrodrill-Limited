How Solar Water Pumping Works

Simple, efficient technology for reliable water access

1

Solar Energy Capture

Solar panels convert sunlight into direct current (DC) electricity throughout the day

2

Power Conversion

Controller converts and regulates power to match pump requirements

3

Water Pumping

Submersible or surface pump moves water from source to storage

4

Water Storage

Water is stored in elevated tanks for gravity-fed distribution

5

Distribution & Use

Water flows to points of use through pipes and taps
