8) FAQ

Is borehole water in Garissa always salty?

Not always, but salinity risk is common in some areas. Water quality varies by exact location and depth, so the safest approach is to measure EC/TDS early and confirm with a full lab analysis before investing in treatment or pump upgrades.

What should I prioritize when drilling a borehole in Garissa?

Prioritize a site-specific survey, written construction specs (casing, gravel pack and sanitary seal), proper development, test pumping with a report, and water testing (EC/TDS, major ions, hardness, iron/manganese, and microbiology if drinking).

How do I plan for reliable water supply in a dry area like Garissa?

Plan for reliability with verified yield (test pumping), adequate storage tanks, correct pump sizing, and realistic daily demand planning. If water quality shows salinity, choose treatment only after lab results confirm the exact chemistry.
