3) Step-by-step drilling process (what a clean job looks like)

    Site review + access planning
    Rig access, water for drilling, and safe setback from septic/latrines.
  • 2

5) Water quality tests to run after drilling (Meru checklist)

  • 2

    Helps predict corrosion or scaling tendency.
  • 3

  • ‚úì

  • ‚úì

9) FAQ

What depth should I expect for a borehole in Meru?

Depth is site-specific. A proper hydrogeological survey helps estimate target aquifers and likely drilling depth. Two nearby plots can still require different depths depending on geology and fracture zones.

Which water quality tests matter most after drilling in Meru?

Start with EC/TDS and pH, then run a lab panel for fluoride, hardness (Ca/Mg), iron/manganese, major ions, and microbiology if water will be used for drinking‚Äîespecially if storage tanks are involved.

What are the biggest cost drivers for drilling a borehole in Meru?

Key cost drivers include depth (meters drilled), casing and gravel pack requirements, test pumping duration, pump capacity/brand, power setup (solar vs grid), and the treatment system (only if lab results confirm a need).
