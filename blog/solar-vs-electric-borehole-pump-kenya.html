Solar vs Electric Borehole Pump in Kenya: Which Is Better?

Choosing a pump system affects long-term cost and reliability. Here’s how to decide based on location, daily water demand, and power availability.

When solar pumping is best

  • Off-grid / unreliable electricity areas
  • Farm irrigation and livestock projects
  • Lower recurring power bills

When electric pumping is best

  • Stable grid power available
  • Higher-flow needs at night (without batteries)
  • Lower upfront equipment cost

System sizing matters

Pump capacity must match borehole yield, total head (height), and daily water needs. Wrong sizing causes low pressure or pump damage.

FAQs

Do solar pumps work on cloudy days?

Yes, but output reduces. Proper storage planning and sizing solves most issues.
