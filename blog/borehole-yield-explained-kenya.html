Borehole Yield Explained (Litres Per Hour)

What “yield” really means

Yield is the water a borehole can produce reliably over time. The key word is sustainable yield.

How yield is measured

  • Test pumping (often extended hours)
  • Recovery rate + drawdown observation
  • Performance reporting

Why yield matters for pump sizing

A pump that exceeds yield can cause excessive drawdown and long-term performance issues. Correct sizing protects the system.
