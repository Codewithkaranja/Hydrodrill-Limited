8) FAQ

How much does it cost to drill a borehole in Kenya in 2026?

Total cost depends on depth, geology, casing requirements, test pumping, and the pump/power/storage setup. The safest way to budget is to get a survey-based depth range and compare itemized quotes that include casing, development, test pumping, and water testing.

What costs are often excluded from borehole drilling quotes?

Common exclusions include casing and gravel pack, sanitary seal, development, test pumping, sampling and lab tests, mobilization/transport, pump installation, electrical/solar setup, storage tanks, and any treatment system.

What is the best way to compare drilling companies?

Compare the same deliverables: drilling rate per meter, casing specs, gravel pack and sanitary seal, development method, test pumping duration and reporting, water testing scope, pump installation scope, warranty and after-sales support.
