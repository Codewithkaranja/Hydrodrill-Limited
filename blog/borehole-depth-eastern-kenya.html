How Deep Should a Borehole Be in Eastern Kenya?

Depth depends on local geology, water-bearing zones, and the yield you need. The safest approach is a proper hydrogeological survey before drilling.

What determines borehole depth?

  • Rock type & fractures (aquifers)
  • Nearby borehole performance
  • Target yield for home/farm/institution
  • Seasonal reliability requirements

Planning tips

  • Do survey first (siting + risk reduction)
  • Size pump after test pumping (not guessing)
  • Plan storage + tower for stable pressure
