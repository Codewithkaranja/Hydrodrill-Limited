6) FAQ

How much does a hydrogeological survey cost in Kenya?

Many projects fall in the KSh 40,000‚Äì110,000+ range depending on access, scope, and whether permit support is included.

What is included in a hydrogeological survey for a borehole?

Typically: site assessment, geophysical siting, recommended drilling point(s), estimated depth range, formation notes, and a written report.

Can I skip the survey and just drill?

Not recommended. Skipping the survey increases risk of low yield, wrong siting, or hidden water chemistry issues that are expensive to treat.

Does a survey guarantee water?

No survey can guarantee a specific yield, but a proper one reduces risk and improves decision quality. Test pumping after drilling is what confirms actual yield.
