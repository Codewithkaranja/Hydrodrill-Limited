Common Borehole Drilling Mistakes in Kenya

  • Skipping hydrogeological survey
  • Low-quality casing and screens
  • Not doing proper well development
  • Skipping test pumping (guessing yield)
  • Wrong pump sizing and no storage planning
  • Ignoring water quality testing

Fix: Follow survey → drill → develop → test pump → size pump → install storage/tower.
