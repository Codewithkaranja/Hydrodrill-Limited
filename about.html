Our Story

Pioneering water solutions in Kenya since 2009

15+ Years Excellence

Leading Water Solutions Provider in Kenya

Hydrodrill & Pump Solutions Limited was founded in 2009 with a vision to revolutionize water access in Kenya through professional borehole drilling and sustainable water solutions. What began as a small drilling operation has grown into one of Kenya's most trusted water solutions companies.

Over the past 15+ years, we have successfully completed over 850 borehole projects across 42 counties, providing reliable water access to thousands of homes, farms, schools, and businesses. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has established us as industry leaders.

Our Journey

Milestones in our commitment to water solutions

2009

Company Foundation

Hydrodrill & Pump Solutions Limited was established in Machakos, Kenya, with a single drilling rig and a vision to provide reliable water solutions.

2012

Expansion & Certification

Acquired Water Resources Management Authority (WRMA) certification and expanded services to include hydrogeological surveys and pump installations.

2015

Nationwide Operations

Expanded operations to serve clients across 25 counties, established regional offices, and invested in modern drilling equipment.

2018

Solar Solutions Division

Launched our solar water pumping division, providing eco-friendly solutions for off-grid and remote locations.

2021

500th Project Milestone

Successfully completed our 500th borehole project and expanded our team to include specialized hydrogeologists and engineers.

2024

Industry Leadership

Recognized as one of Kenya's leading water solutions providers with 850+ projects completed and nationwide coverage.

Our Mission & Vision

Driving sustainable water access across Kenya

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable, and sustainable water solutions through professional drilling, innovative pump systems, and customer-focused service. We are committed to ensuring every Kenyan has access to clean, reliable water through our expertise in groundwater development.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's leading water solutions provider, recognized for technical excellence, environmental responsibility, and transformative impact on communities. We envision a Kenya where water scarcity is eliminated through sustainable groundwater management.
