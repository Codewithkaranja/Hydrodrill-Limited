Our Story

Pioneering water solutions in Kenya since 2014

12+ Years Excellence

Leading Water Solutions Provider in Kenya

Hydrodrill & Pump Solutions Limited was founded in 2014 with a vision to revolutionize water access in Kenya through professional borehole drilling and sustainable water solutions. What began as a small drilling operation has grown into one of Kenya's most trusted water solutions companies.

Over the past 12+ years, we have successfully completed over 500 borehole projects across 42 counties, providing reliable water access to thousands of homes, farms, schools, and businesses. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has established us as industry leaders.

Our Journey

Milestones in our commitment to water solutions

2014

Company Founded

Hydro Drill and Pump Solutions Limited was founded in September 2014 as a private company to deliver dependable water and pumping solutions.

2018

Registration & Compliance

Fully registered and incorporated as a Limited Company under the Companies Act (CAP 486) with registration number PVT-27UK6LZ, establishing a strong legal and operational foundation.

2019

Service Portfolio Growth

Expanded to offer geological surveys, borehole drilling, pump supply and installation, plumbing works, steel tank and tower construction, and general supplies.

2020

Solar Pumping Solutions

Spearheaded the adoption of solar products and solarized electrical pumps—helping clients achieve efficient and cost-effective water systems.

2023

500+ Boreholes Milestone

Successfully drilled and equipped 500+ boreholes with durable, high-performance products—delivering reliable results for clients.

Today

Trusted Team & Regional Vision

Powered by a highly qualified team committed to integrity and excellence, we currently serve clients across Kenya and aim to expand across Eastern Africa.
